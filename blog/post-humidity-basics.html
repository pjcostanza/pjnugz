Grow note: dialing in humidity (basics)

A quick, non-technical walkthrough to keep your environment stable.

Educational content only. Not medical advice. Follow local laws.

Why humidity matters

Stable air helps consistency. Big swings can stress plants and invite issues.

Simple setup

  1. Use a basic hygrometer (place it at canopy height).
  2. Run a small humidifier/dehumidifier as needed.
  3. Adjust gradually; change one variable at a time.
